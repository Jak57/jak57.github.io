About
Hello! I am a Junior Software Engineer at REVE Systems, where I work under the guidance of Md. Maniruzzaman Monir and Nafiul Alam Fuji. I hold a B.Sc. degree from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, where I had the privilege of being advised by Md. Shahidur Rahman.

At REVE Systems, I am primarily involved in the R&D department, focusing on real-time video conferencing applications. My work emphasizes audio processing, specifically in areas such as audio mixing, noise reduction, acoustic feedback loop detection, and echo cancellation. Previously, I contributed to projects including the development of a Bangla neural lemmatizer, text-to-IPA transcription, automatic speech recognition (ASR) for regional dialects, and image classification.
